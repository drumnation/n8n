## Service 1: Google OAuth

**Goal:** Get Client ID and Client Secret for OAuth 2.0

**Steps:**

1. **Navigate to:** https://console.cloud.google.com
2. **Wait** for page to load (3-5 seconds)
3. **Check:** Do you see "Google Cloud" logo?
   - If NO: Stop and tell me
   - If YES: Continue

4. **Create Project:**
   - Click "Select a project" dropdown (near top)
   - Click "NEW PROJECT"
   - Wait for new page to load
   - In "Project name" field, enter: "n8n Workflows"
   - Click "CREATE"
   - **Wait 10 seconds** for project to be created

5. **Select New Project:**
   - Click "Select a project" dropdown again
   - Click "n8n Workflows"
   - Wait for project to load

6. **Open API Library:**
   - Click hamburger menu (â‰¡) in top-left
   - Hover/click "APIs & Services"
   - Click "Library"
   - Wait for library page to load

7. **Enable Gmail API:**
   - In search box, type: "Gmail API"
   - Click the "Gmail API" result
   - Click "ENABLE" button
   - Wait for it to say "API enabled" (3-5 seconds)

8. **Enable Google Sheets API:**
   - Click "â† Back" or navigate back to library
   - Search: "Google Sheets API"
   - Click result
   - Click "ENABLE"
   - Wait for confirmation

9. **Enable Google Calendar API:**
   - Back to library
   - Search: "Google Calendar API"
   - Click result
   - Click "ENABLE"
   - Wait for confirmation

10. **Go to Credentials:**
    - Click hamburger menu (â‰¡)
    - Click "APIs & Services"
    - Click "Credentials"
    - Wait for page to load

11. **Configure OAuth Consent Screen** (if prompted):
    - If you see "CONFIGURE CONSENT SCREEN" button, click it
    - Select "External" radio button
    - Click "CREATE"
    - Wait for form to load

12. **Fill Consent Screen Form:**
    - App name: "n8n Workflows"
    - User support email: Select from dropdown (should be my email)
    - Scroll down
    - Developer contact email: Enter my email address
    - Click "SAVE AND CONTINUE"

13. **Add Scopes:**
    - Click "ADD OR REMOVE SCOPES"
    - In the popup, add these scopes (search for each and check the box):
      - https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send
      - https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly
      - https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets
      - https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar
    - Click "UPDATE"
    - Click "SAVE AND CONTINUE"

14. **Add Test User:**
    - Click "ADD USERS"
    - Enter my Gmail address
    - Click "ADD"
    - Click "SAVE AND CONTINUE"

15. **Back to Dashboard:**
    - Click "BACK TO DASHBOARD"
    - In left sidebar, click "Credentials"

16. **Create OAuth Client ID:**
    - Click "CREATE CREDENTIALS" button (top)
    - Select "OAuth client ID"
    - Wait for form

17. **Configure OAuth Client:**
    - Application type: Select "Web application"
    - Name: "n8n OAuth Client"
    - Scroll to "Authorized redirect URIs"
    - Click "ADD URI"
    - Paste exactly: http://localhost:5678/rest/oauth2-credential/callback
    - Click "CREATE" at bottom

18. **Extract Credentials:**
    - A popup will show "OAuth client created"
    - **COPY the Client ID** (starts with number, ends with .apps.googleusercontent.com)
    - **COPY the Client Secret** (random string)
    - Store these for final report
    - Click "OK"

**Checkpoint:** Confirm you have:
- Client ID: (number).apps.googleusercontent.com
- Client Secret: random string
